What even is BMT??



BMT (short for BatMans-Towing) is a virtual Company in the game "Motor Town Behind the Wheel".

Constisting of 16 people from all over the world, we are a Company with amazing Staff and Operators that help other players in need of towing or recovery services.

We pride ourselves on our fast response times, professionalism, and dedication to customer satisfaction.

Whether you're stuck on the side of the road or need a vehicle transported, BMT is here to help!

if you need assistance, don't hesitate to reach out to one of our operators in-game or via game chat or the TWL Discord server.

Every vehicle is important to us, and we are committed to providing top-notch service to all our clients.

When you choose BMT, you can rest assured that your vehicle is in good hands.

We hope to see you on the road soon!
